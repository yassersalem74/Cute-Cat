Cats types

Let's get Knowing with the types of cats

Persian Cat

The Persian cat is a long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle. It is also known as the "Persian Longhair" in English-speaking countries.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is a large domesticated cat breed. It is one of the oldest natural breeds in North America. The breed originated in the U.S. state of Maine.

Munchkin cat

The Munchkin is a relatively new breed of cat characterized by its very short legs, which are caused by genetic mutation, is considered to be the original breed of dwarf cat.

Kinkalow

A kinkalow is a playful and curious cat, who can enjoy many active cat toys,considered as great choice for younger kids because they aren't aggressive, or attention demanding.
